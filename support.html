How do I unlock games? ‚ñº
Most games cost 50 coins to unlock. Some special games require gems instead. Click on a locked game to see its unlock requirements. You earn coins by playing games (100 coins every 30 seconds) or through gambling games like Mines, Blackjack, and Poker.
What are gems and how do I get them? ‚ñº
Gems are premium currency earned through the Prestige system. When you rebirth, you convert your coins into gems. Gems can be used to unlock special games, purchase exclusive cosmetics in the shop, and buy powerful upgrades like Auto Collector and Coin Magnet.
How does the Prestige system work? ‚ñº
Click the "PRESTIGE ‚ú®" button to access the rebirth system. You can convert your coins into gems at different tiers. Higher tiers give better conversion rates but require more coins. After rebirthing, your coin balance resets but you keep your gems and unlocked games.
